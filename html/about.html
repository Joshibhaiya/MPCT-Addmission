our history

Pratap Vahini Samaj Kalyan Sansthan, Gwalior was incepted in 1995 after the due registration from Registrar, Firms & Society, M.P. Govt. The society was established by Lt. Shri Ram Singh Dhakre and his associates. The society established the first Engg. College of the region in 1996 on the name of great Indian legendry Maharana Pratap College of Technology, which is now the parent organization of the group society under the leadership of Lt. Shri Ram Singh Dhakre finished its second venture by establishing first Dental College of the region in 2003, this college again has set now dimension in the field of medical education. After this in late 20s the society promoted and established nursing college , Allied Science College and Management institute.
